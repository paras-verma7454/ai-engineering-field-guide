AI & Automation Engineer (3 Roles to build Workato Workflow Automation for Sales, Marketing, and G&A) – Hyderabad, India

The Opportunity

Nasuni is hiring 3 AI & Automation Engineers, one each focused on Sales, Marketing, and G&A, to join our Enterprise Business Systems team in Hyderabad.

If you are passionate about building intelligent automations, AI agents, and modern integrations creating significant business impact, this is your chance to own outcomes, shape strategy, and build at enterprise scale.

You will work at the center of Nasuni’s next generation of automation, leveraging Workato, Agentic AI, LLMs, MCPs, and modern SaaS ecosystems, to help our business grow faster, operate smarter, and scale globally.

Primary Responsibilities:

What You’ll Do (All 3 Roles)

  • Design, develop, test, and deploy integrations, automations, and AI agents using Workato (including Agent Studio & Workato GO).
  • Translate business requirements into scalable workflows, recipes, and agentic solutions.
  • Partner with global teams and incorporate feedback effectively across time zones.
  • Adhere to governance standards and engineering best practices.
  • Recommend and implement process improvements aligned to enterprise architecture.

Role Specializations

  1. AI & Automation Engineer – Sales

Help automate and accelerate the revenue engine.
Build solutions that support Salesforce, Gainsight, Clari, Gong, Tackle, Workspan, Zift, and more.
Ideal for someone who loves optimizing sales workflows, pipeline development, and data-driven forecasting.

  1. AI & Automation Engineer – Marketing

Empower Marketing with intelligent automation across HubSpot, Google Analytics, 6Sense (or similar), and modern MarTech ecosystems.
Perfect for someone excited about AI-powered pipeline generation, attribution, and marketing analytics.

  1. AI & Automation Engineer – G&A

Improve efficiency across HR, Finance, Accounting, and Operations.
Work with tools like Outlook, Zoom, Slack, Greenhouse, procurement/expense systems, and core corporate apps.
Great for someone who enjoys company-wide impact and cross-functional optimization.

What You Bring / Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Accounting, Finance, Computer Science or Engineering or related field, or similar work experience.
  • 1 to 3+ Years in Workflow Automation or similar Enterprise SaaS AI Automation environments
  • 3 to 5+ Years overall experience.   
  • Experience integrating and automating business systems that support Sales, Marketing, Accounting, or Finance and a good understanding of how the software is used by the business.
  • Completed or Planning to complete Workato Foundations Level 1 & 2 + Workato Agentic AI Basics  
  • Workato Foundations Courses can be taken here:  https://academy.workato.com/page/workato-foundations
  • Demonstrated expertise in designing and using data object models and relational and non-relational databases, with a track record of delivering data integrations that reliably move and transform data between systems.
  • Familiarity with LLMs, MCPs, and AI-driven automation patterns
  • Demonstrated in-depth knowledge of APIs, JSON formatting, and SQL for building and maintaining integrations.
  • Skilled in Python, JavaScript, or Ruby, with additional OO languages (e.g., Java, C#) considered a plus for building AI automation systems.
  • Experience with Ruby (Workato’s core language) and proficiency in Python and JavaScript to build and extend automation connectors efficiently.
  • Experience integrating modern SaaS platforms for Sales, Marketing, Finance, Accounting, or HR Systems.

Preferred

  • Hands-on experience with Workato Agent Studio, Workato GO, and MCP servers
  • Experience with other IPaas systems like Mulesoft, Boomi, or others is helpful.
  • Background in high-growth B2B SaaS (Ability to iterate quickly, meet deadlines, and release tools in agile sprints)
  • Able to think critically and independently to make decisions and deliver high-quality code efficiently.
  • Additional Workato AI certification
  • Experience with cloud storage platforms such as Nasuni, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, AWS, Snowflake, or similar systems is a plus.

About Nasuni

Nasuni is a profitable, growing SaaS data infrastructure company reinventing enterprise file storage and data management in an AI-driven world. We power the data infrastructure of the world’s most innovative enterprises. Backed by Vista Equity Partners, our engineers are not working behind the scenes, they’re building what’s next with AI. Our platform lets businesses seamlessly store, access, protect, and unlock AI-driven insights from exploding volumes of unstructured file data. As an engineer here, you will help build AI-powered infrastructure trusted by 900+ global customers, including Dow, Mattel, and Autodesk.

Nasuni is headquartered in Boston, USA with offices in Cork-Ireland, London-UK and we are starting an India Innovation Center in Hyderabad India to leverage exuberant IT talent available in India. The company’s recent Annual Revenue is at $160M and is growing at 25% CAGR. 

We have a hybrid work culture. 3 days a week working from the Hyderabad office during core working hours and 2 days working from home.

Nasuni is transforming how the world’s largest enterprises manage unstructured file data with a next-generation, AI-ready platform. Backed by Vista Equity Partners, we combine the strength of a market-leading SaaS company with the operational excellence, scale, and innovation rigor of one of the world’s top technology investment firms.

Our mission:
Turn massive unstructured data file stores into actionable, secure, AI-ready data, enabling automation, analytics, and intelligent business outcomes.

What We Do

Nasuni is a leading unstructured data platform for enterprises where file data is mission-critical for both people and AI. We power the operational file layer where work happens — helping organizations manage, protect, and activate data so teams can work smarter, reduce costs, and operate securely without limits. Built on a patented architecture that fuses cloud object storage with enterprise file services — including permissions, versioning, and a global namespace — Nasuni delivers high-performance file access, global data availability, and a scalable, governed, AI-ready single source of truth across every major cloud. Trusted by more than 1,300 enterprises globally, Nasuni helps organizations modernize file infrastructure, strengthen data security, and support AI-driven operations. Learn more at www.nasuni.com.
