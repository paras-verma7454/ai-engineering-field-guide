About Oleria

    Oleria provides adaptive and autonomous identity security solutions to protect and enable today's dynamic enterprise. Our platform delivers unparalleled fine-grained access visibility, including usage insights at the individual resource level, so CISOs can confidently answer: Who has access to what? How did they get it? What are they doing with it?

    With our recent launch of Adaptive Identity Governance, we have a massive opportunity to disrupt the legacy IGA market. We're replacing multi-year, multi-million dollar deployments with a data-first, AI-native platform that gets organizations governed in under an hour. It's a wide-open market, and we're just getting started.

    Founded by cybersecurity veterans Jim Alkove and Jagadeesh Kunda, who built and secured platforms used by billions globally, we've raised $60M+ from Evolution Equity Partners, Salesforce Ventures, Tapestry VC, Zscaler, and other technology luminaries. Our Fortune 500 and public sector clients trust us to protect millions of users worldwide.

    We're proud to be recognized as a LinkedIn Best Startup and a Best Startup to Work For by Built In. Join a well-funded team with real traction, a game-changing new product, and a mission to deliver identity security that sets your business free.

    Learn more: www.oleria.com

The Mission

    Oleria has proven the product with Fortune 500 customers and $60M+ in funding. Now we need to engineer the go-to-market machine. You’re the person who builds it.

    You design, build, and operate the AI-powered technical systems that power our pipeline — from signal detection through deal acceleration. This isn’t a marketing role with a technical veneer. You write code, deploy AI agents, build data pipelines, and create the automation infrastructure that lets a lean GTM team punch way above its weight.

    A sales rep who walks into every meeting with AI-generated account research, real-time competitive intel, and a prospect-specific value narrative — that’s your system at work. You make that possible.

What You Ship

    Pipeline Intelligence Infrastructure

  • Data foundation for our two-path pipeline model: founder/partner-sourced deals and marketing-sourced leads running the full funnel

  • Enrichment workflows, ICP scoring models, and signal-based triggers that surface the right accounts at the right time

  • Integration of Clay, AI agents, and third-party data sources into a unified account intelligence layer

    • Result: Sales team spends zero time on manual account research

    AI-Native Outbound Systems

  • AI-powered outbound engines targeting CISOs, IAM leaders, and identity security buyers across enterprise and state/federal accounts

  • Personalized campaign systems that generate, test, and optimize messaging variants at scale — programmatically, not manually

  • Automated A/B testing infrastructure across channels, messaging, and timing

    • Result: 10x messaging variant velocity vs. industry standard

    CRM & RevOps Automation

  • Automated lead routing, opportunity management, forecasting inputs, and handoff workflows

  • Connected GTM stack: CRM, enrichment, sequencing, and analytics in one coherent system

  • Data quality enforcement — clean, current, and actionable pipeline data at all times

    • Result: End-to-end pipeline visibility with zero manual data entry

    Sales Enablement Tooling

  • Automated competitive intelligence updates and AI-generated account research briefs

  • Dynamic battle cards and prospect-specific leave-behinds

  • Internal tools that accelerate every stage of the sales cycle

    • Result: Sales team never walks into a meeting underprepared

    Measurement & Feedback Loops

  • Dashboards tracking pipeline velocity, conversion rates, campaign attribution, and system health

  • Feedback loops so the GTM machine gets smarter with every cycle

  • Data pipelines connecting marketing, sales, and product signals

    • Result: Every GTM decision backed by real-time data

What We Look For

  • 3–5 years as a full-stack or application engineer, with strong front-end skills (React/Next.js preferred)

  • Experience building customer-facing tools, interactive experiences, or PLG features—not just internal dashboards

  • Comfortable with data: APIs, ETL pipelines, database design, and integrating multiple data sources

  • Experience with or strong interest in Clay, Segment, or similar GTM data platforms

  • You build fast. You ship MVPs in days, iterate based on data, and don’t wait for perfect requirements

  • You use AI coding tools (Cursor, Copilot, Claude) as a daily multiplier

  • Interest in cybersecurity and identity—you don’t need to be an expert, but you’re curious about the space

Nice to Have

  • Experience building PLG tools at a B2B SaaS company

  • Familiarity with Salesforce/Pardot data models

  • Experience with AI/ML for scoring, classification, or recommendation systems

  • Background in developer tools, security tools, or enterprise infrastructure

  • Experience with AI/ML for scoring, classification, or recommendation systems

  • Background building PLG tools, internal tools, or developer platforms at a B2B SaaS company

  • Prior work at a Series A–C startup where you wore multiple hats
