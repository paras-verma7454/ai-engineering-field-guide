The Role
Design, build and deploy AI applications for utilities. Responsibilities include working with LLMs, data pipelines, and ensuring system performance.

Company Overview:

For more than 50 years, NISC has worked to develop technology solutions for our customers, who we call our “Members”. Those Members are comprised primarily of 960+ energy (utility) and communication (broadband) organizations across North America. Our mission is to deliver technology solutions and services that are Member-focused, quality driven and valued priced. Our Members have over 18 million end customers (residential and businesses who receive power, internet, television and/or telephone services) that our enterprise software solution enables our Member's employees to compete effectively in the industry, while excelling in providing customer service to their end customers.   

NISC exists to serve our Members and help them serve their communities through our innovative software products, services and outstanding customer support. We are an AI-forward company committed to being a technology leader in our industry. NISC has been ranked in ComputerWorld’s Best Places to Work for 23 years, and we are looking for qualified individuals to join our team.  

Position Overview: 

We are looking for a high-agency, AI-native Software Engineer who loves building real-world AI systems that make a measurable difference. 

This is not a research role. This is not a “play with models in a lab” role. This is a builder role. 

You will design, build, ship, and continuously improve production-grade AI applications that are embedded directly into mission-critical software used by utilities and broadband providers across North America. Your work will power customer service interactions, operational workflows, and intelligent automation at scale. 

We are looking for someone who: 

  • Gets excited about taking AI from idea → prototype → production → scale
  • Loves experimenting with new models, frameworks, and agentic patterns
  • Has a “there’s a way” mindset when solving hard technical problems
  • Believes AI should drive tangible business impact, not just technical novelty
  • Thrives in ambiguity and moves fast while maintaining engineering discipline 

At NISC, AI is not a side initiative — it is a core strategic priority. You will help shape how AI transforms the way our Members serve their communities. 

If you’ve built and deployed AI systems that real users depend on — and you want to do it in an organization that values ownership, innovation, and meaningful impact — we encourage you to apply. 

Essential Functions: 

  • Design & Build Production AI Systems: Architect, develop, and deploy AI-powered features and applications using LLMs, RAG pipelines, agentic workflows, and modern ML techniques.
  • Own Outcomes, Not Just Code: Take ideas from concept to production. Prototype quickly, validate with users, iterate based on feedback, and optimize for reliability and performance.
  • LLM & Agent Integration: Integrate foundation models, fine-tuned models, and multi-agent systems into enterprise-grade applications in secure, scalable environments.
  • Data & Retrieval Engineering: Design and optimize data ingestion pipelines, embeddings workflows, and vector database implementations that support high-quality AI responses.
  • Performance & Scalability: Deploy AI systems that are resilient, observable, and cost-efficient in production environments.
  • Mission Alignment: Ensure solutions are practical, intuitive, and aligned with the operational realities of cooperatives, utilities, and broadband providers.
  • Continuous Innovation: Actively explore new AI capabilities and rapidly evaluate how they can improve our products and development practices.
  • Collaborative Execution: Partner with product, architecture, and engineering teams to embed AI capabilities across our enterprise platform.
  • Follow all established software development methodologies and procedures.
  • Other duties as assigned  

Desired Experience & Skills: 

  • 8-10+ years experience in software development, preferably with at least 3+ years in AI/ML software development.
  • Demonstrated experience shipping AI solutions used by real customers — not just prototypes or academic projects
  • Experience integrating LLMs into applications (prompt engineering, evaluation, fine-tuning, API orchestration)
  • Experience designing RAG pipelines, embeddings workflows, and working with vector databases
  • Experience deploying AI systems in cloud environments (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Technical Expertise:  
    • Strong Python proficiency and familiarity with ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, or equivalent)
    • Experience building scalable APIs and backend services
    • Understanding of agentic architectures, orchestration frameworks, or multi-agent systems
    • Experience optimizing AI performance, cost, and latency in production
    • Familiarity with observability, evaluation frameworks, and model monitoring
  • Mindset & Attributes (Critical for Success)
    • Builder mentality — you love shipping
    • High ownership — you see problems and move toward them
    • Curiosity about emerging AI capabilities and how to apply them pragmatically
    • Bias toward action and experimentation
    • Comfort operating in evolving AI landscapes
    • Ability to balance speed with engineering discipline
    • Strong communicator who can translate AI capabilities into business value
  • Bonus Experience
    • Experience in utilities, broadband, cooperatives, or enterprise SaaS
    • Knowledge of AI safety, evaluation strategies, or explainability techniques
    • Full-stack experience or ability to deliver end-to-end AI features
    • Portfolio, GitHub, or case studies demonstrating deployed AI systems
  • Experience working with utilities, broadband providers, or cooperatives, preferred.
  • Commitment to NISC’s Statement of Shared Values

  • Innovation – We promote the spirit of creativity and champion new ideas.
  • Teamwork – We exemplify the cooperative spirit by working together.
  • Empowerment – We believe individuals have the power to make a difference.
  • Personal Development – We believe the free exchange of knowledge and information is absolutely necessary to the success of each individual and the organization.

  • Hybrid from one of our office locations:

What We Do

National Information Solutions Cooperative (NISC) is an information technology organization that develops, implements and supports software and hardware solutions for our Members/Customers. We deliver advanced solutions, services and support to more than 870 independent telephone companies, electric cooperatives and other public power entities in all 50 states, American Samoa, Palau and Canada. NISC is an industry leader providing information technology solutions including billing, accounting, operations, automated mailroom services, third-party integration as well as many other solutions. With facilities in Mandan, N.D., Lake Saint Louis, Mo., Cedar Rapids, Iowa, Shawano, Wis., Blacksburg, Va. and Austin, Texas, NISC and its subsidiaries employ more than 1,300 professionals between the six locations and at virtual office sites around the country. Additional information can be found at www.nisc.coop or by calling: 866.999.6472.
