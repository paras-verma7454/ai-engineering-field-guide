Preferred Job Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of LLM architecture, inference, context limits, and cost/latency tradeoffs. 
  • Mastery of agent prompting frameworks like ReAct, Chain‑of‑Thought, structured outputs (JSON), and multi-step reasoning patterns. 
  • Experience building agentic RAG systems (iterative retrieval, self‑evaluation, strategy switching). 

·         Hands‑on experience with LangChain, AutoGen, CrewAI, or similar agentic frameworks. 

·         Familiarity with multi-agent orchestration, tool use, and autonomous workflow design. 

·         Understanding of agent ecosystem components: reasoning engines, memory systems, tool interfaces, and goal management.

·         Proficiency with ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch) and reinforcement learning concepts. 

·         Experience implementing LLMs with vector databases, embeddings, and RAG pipelines.

·         Ability to evaluate model performance, mitigate hallucinations, and design safe action loops.
