The Role
The role involves designing and building cloud-native applications, enhancing AI capabilities, and collaborating with teams in secure environments.
Summary Generated by Built In
Location Designation: Hybrid - 3 days per week
We are seeking a Software Engineer with experience in generative and agentic AI to help design and deliver modern, cloud-native insurance platforms. This role sits within our Core Systems team and partners closely with engineers, architects, data scientists, and product teams to build scalable, secure, and highly available solutions leveraging AWS, APIs, DevSecOps, and AI. You will contribute to advancing automation, developer productivity, and AI-enabled capabilities while continuing to grow your technical expertise within enterprise engineering standards and best practices.
What You'll Do:
  • Design, build, test, and deploy cloud-native applications and services on AWS under the guidance of senior engineers and architects
  • Contribute to AI and automation initiatives that enhance developer productivity and platform capabilities
  • Support the development and maintenance of CI/CD pipelines and DevSecOps automation
  • Build and consume APIs and reusable services
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code following engineering best practices
  • Ensure solutions meet security, performance, resilience, and compliance standards
  • Participate in Agile ceremonies and collaborate with cross-functional teams
  • Contribute to documentation, technical discussions, and continuous improvement initiatives

What You'll Bring:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
  • 1-3 years of software engineering experience building and supporting production systems
  • Hands-on experience with AWS and cloud-native application development
  • Familiarity with Infrastructure as Code (e.g., Terraform)
  • Exposure to CI/CD pipelines and DevSecOps practices
  • Proficiency in at least one modern programming language (e.g., Python, JavaScript, TypeScript)
  • Experience building generative or agentic AI applications
  • Familiarity with APIs, containerization concepts (EKS, ECR), and modern development tools (e.g., GitHub, Jira)
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work effectively in a team environment
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

Preferred Qualifications
  • Exposure to AI/ML through academic, project, or professional experience
  • Experience with full-stack development of AI-powered tools using modern technologies (e.g., React, Node.js, LangChain, LangGraph)
  • Experience contributing to automation or developer productivity initiatives
  • Experience in financial services, insurance, or wealth management
  • Relevant certifications or coursework in cloud, DevOps, or AI

What We Do

At New York Life, our 180-year legacy of integrity, mutuality, and financial strength fuels a future defined by bold transformation. As the largest mutual life insurance company in the U.S., we operate on behalf of our policy owners—not shareholders. That structure allows us to take a long-term view, investing in people, purpose, and innovation that endures. Guided by a clear enterprise vision to become a technology-, data-, and AI-powered company, we’re modernizing our platforms, rearchitecting experiences, and embedding intelligence across our products and services. Our mission has always been about helping people through life’s most meaningful moments. Today, technology is amplifying that mission—enabling us to serve clients, advisors, and communities in more personalized, proactive ways. With a diversified business portfolio spanning insurance, investments, retirement, group benefits, and direct-to-consumer offerings, New York Life delivers the stability of a Fortune 100 company with the agility of one that’s continuously evolving. We’re powered by a values-led culture, inclusive teams, and a shared belief that when our people thrive, so does our company. Here, tradition fuels momentum—and your ideas, energy, and growth power what’s next.

Why Work With Us

New York Life is transforming from the inside out—blending 180 years of trust with the velocity of innovation. What makes us different is our culture: grounded in integrity, humanity, and shared success—values that show up in how we work, lead, and grow. If you want a place where innovation has purpose—build what's next with us.
