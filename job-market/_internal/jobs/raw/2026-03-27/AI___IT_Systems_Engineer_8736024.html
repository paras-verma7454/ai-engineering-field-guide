Jasper is the leading AI marketing platform, enabling the world's most innovative companies to reimagine their end-to-end marketing workflows and drive higher ROI through increased brand consistency, efficiency, and personalization at scale.

Jasper has been recognized as "one of the Top 15 Most Innovative AI Companies of 2024" by Fast Company and is trusted by nearly 20% of the Fortune 500 – including Prudential, Ulta Beauty, and Wayfair. Founded in 2021, Jasper is a remote-first organization with team members across the US, France, and Australia.

About The Role

As Jasper undergoes an agentic AI shift, this role is critical for ensuring our IT infrastructure is robust, secure, and fine-tuned to handle advanced AI workflows. Spending 70-80% of your time directly on AI enablement, you will be deployed across the organization to tackle hands-on technical implementations, bridging the gap between traditional IT systems management and cutting-edge AI deployments. You will design and deploy intelligent systems that automate cross-platform workflows, preparing Jasper for AI-driven scale by enabling agentic AI to trigger real actions across our entire tech stack.

This fully remote role reports to the Director of AI Enablement & IT and is open to candidates located anywhere in the continental US.

 What you will do at Jasper

  • Agentic IT Infrastructure & Automation: Modernize and fine-tune our IT systems to ensure they securely and efficiently support autonomous and agentic AI workflows. Build reliable, scalable automation infrastructure that significantly enhances team efficiency and reduces manual work.

  • AI Implementation & Cross-Platform Workflows: Partner with the Director of AI Enablement & IT to operationalize AI initiatives using tools like Claude, ChatGPT, and Zapier. Design intelligent workflows that connect platforms like Google Workspace and Slack, allowing AI agents to trigger real, multi-step actions.

  • Core Systems Management: Administer and optimize core IT domains, including Identity Providers and Mobile Device Management. Leverage advanced features like Okta Workflows to streamline identity and access operations.

  • Cross-Functional Technical Support: Deploy across various departments to assist directly with the technical rollout of AI enablement projects.

  • SaaS Ecosystem Operations: Manage and streamline our broader SaaS stack, including Google Workspace, Linear, and tens of other applications.

  • Enablement & Training: Assist in creating playbooks and training resources to help teams adopt new AI tools seamlessly into their existing workflows.

What you will bring to Jasper

  • 4–6 years of experience in IT systems engineering, IT support, or a related technical role.

  • Demonstrated experience designing and deploying intelligent systems that automate cross-platform workflows and prepare infrastructure for AI-driven scale.

  • Strong, hands-on administrative experience with Identity Providers (Okta) and Mobile Device Management (Rippling IT).

  • Proven track record of managing and integrating modern SaaS applications (Google Workspace, Linear, etc.).

  • Ability to act as a multifaceted technical specialist, comfortably pivoting between core IT operations and cross-functional AI implementation projects.

  • Strong communication skills with the ability to translate complex technical concepts and business impact clearly to non-technical stakeholders.

What you need to know about the Los Angeles Tech Scene

Los Angeles is a global leader in entertainment, so it’s no surprise that many of the biggest players in streaming, digital media and game development call the city home. But the city boasts plenty of non-entertainment innovation as well, with tech companies spanning verticals like AI, fintech, e-commerce and biotech. With major universities like Caltech, UCLA, USC and the nearby UC Irvine, the city has a steady supply of top-flight tech and engineering talent — not counting the graduates flocking to Los Angeles from across the world to enjoy its beaches, culture and year-round temperate climate.

Key Facts About Los Angeles Tech

  • Number of Tech Workers: 375,800; 5.5% of overall workforce (2024 CompTIA survey)
  • Major Tech Employers: Snap, Netflix, SpaceX, Disney, Google
  • Key Industries: Artificial intelligence, adtech, media, software, game development
  • Funding Landscape: $11.6 billion in venture capital funding in 2024 (Pitchbook)
  • Notable Investors: Strong Ventures, Fifth Wall, Upfront Ventures, Mucker Capital, Kittyhawk Ventures
  • Research Centers and Universities: California Institute of Technology, UCLA, University of Southern California, UC Irvine, Pepperdine, California Institute for Immunology and Immunotherapy, Center for Quantum Science and Engineering
