About Pallet

Pallet is building AI Agents to transform the $12 trillion logistics industry, backed by $50M from investors including General Catalyst, Bessemer Venture Partners, and Bain Capital Ventures. Our mission is to automate the physical economy by creating the most reliable intelligence infrastructure for logistics.

Our agents excel at handling complex logistics workflows—scheduling, data entry, and load management—for Fortune 500 companies. But this is only the beginning. Our vision is to be the go-to answer for AI in logistics: from proprietary models to a suite of tools that turn messy business rules into production-ready context for LLMs.

We're positioned to build the next $10B company in logistics. Join us and work alongside leaders from Google, DoorDash, YC, and more to shape the future of logistics tech.

About the Opportunity

Most engineering roles ask you to build in a controlled environment. This one puts you in the field. We're hiring one of our founding team of Agent Delivery Software Engineers to embed directly with customers and deploy production AI agents inside some of the world's largest logistics companies. You will sit alongside customer engineers and operations teams, reverse engineer legacy systems, design pragmatic integrations, and ship AI workflows into messy, real-world environments.

This is not a research or pure backend role. This is a hands-on deployment role focused on getting systems live, keeping them reliable, and owning real-world outcomes.

Why this role is different
  • One of the founding Agent Delivery Engineers at Pallet, with ownership over real customer outcomes from day one
  • Hands-on deployment work across some of the largest logistics companies in the world
  • Direct collaboration with founders and a tight engineering team, no layers, no red tape
  • Meaningful equity in a company targeting a $10B+ outcome
  • Compensation and equity at the 80th percentile, plus bonuses tied to customer go-lives
How You Will Make an Impact
  • Reverse engineer undocumented APIs, ERPs, TMS systems, and internal tools with minimal guidance
  • Debug broken data pipelines, mismatched schemas, and flaky integrations in production environments
  • Sit onsite with customer teams to diagnose failures and ship fixes in real time (roughly 25% travel)
  • Make pragmatic tradeoffs to get systems live under tight timelines
  • Own customer go-lives end-to-end, from scoping to production stability
  • Build with observability in mind so issues surface quickly and stay fixed
Preferred Experience
  • 2 to 4+ years of experience shipping and owning production systems, ideally in a high-growth or early-stage environment
  • Strong experience integrating with third-party APIs, authentication systems, and event-driven workflows
  • Comfortable debugging distributed systems in production under real customer constraints
  • Thrives in ambiguous environments where requirements evolve and documentation is sparse
  • Some experience working directly with customers or external stakeholders is a plus
  • Genuinely enjoys messy real-world systems more than clean internal abstractions
  • Excited to work in person 5 days a week and comfortable with roughly 25% travel to customer sites
  • Not the right fit for those primarily interested in ML research, deeply scoped platform work, or remote-first environments
How we work
  • Impact from day one: your first week might involve wiring a new integration into production or debugging a workflow that saves a customer hours
  • One standup at 10 am PST. The rest is building
  • Small PRs, fast iteration, and a team that sits side by side so questions get answered in minutes
  • Catered lunch daily, Thursday boba runs, and AI tools actively encouraged (Claude, Cursor) as long as you understand the architecture underneath
  • We promote from within: several teammates earned promotions in under a year
Our Tech Stack
  • Backend: Node.js + TypeScript on Encore.dev, event-driven design with message passing and queues, hosted on GCP
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • LLMs: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google (foundation models), fine-tuned models on Vertex AI
  • Browser Automation: Playwright
  • Frontend: Next.js + React + TypeScript, hosted on Vercel
  • Observability: Datadog
Interview Process
  • Intro Chat with Recruiting - (30 mins)
  • Technical Screen + Meet and Greet with an Engineer (60 mins)
  • Executive Chat with Bizops Lead or CTO (30-45 mins)
  • Final Interview: deep-dive technical and behavioral interviews (~4 hours)
Location
  • San Francisco: Since we're a small team building 0-1 products, we prefer candidates to be comfortable working in an in-office environment in San Francisco. We typically come into the office 5 days a week. Relocation assistance can be provided for interested candidates.
  • New York: Satellite Office with ~8 Pallet employees across engineering, operations, and success - we are seeking to hire and grow out the team more here. The team typically comes into the office 4-5 days a week. Relocation assistance is not provided for our New York office.
  • Travel: ~25% travel to customer locations
Compensation

The estimated salary range for this role is $130,000–$200,000, depending on experience and skill set. In addition to base salary, we offer competitive equity, benefits, and growth opportunities. Final compensation will be determined based on a combination of factors, including experience, qualifications, and location.

What We Do

Pallet makes supply chains more efficient. Our AI workforce, CoPallet, handles high-volume tasks like order entry, portal updates, and quoting — all with human-level accuracy and oversight. People who want to make decisions, talk to users, and shape how our AI agents evolve.
